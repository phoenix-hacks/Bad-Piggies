About AIDO

AI video editing is transforming the way videos are created and edited. By leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning, these tools automate tasks, enhance creativity, and personalize the viewing experience. This technology is rapidly changing the landscape for everyone from casual creators to professional filmmakers.

Here's what sets us apart from the rest:

Free and Open Source

AIDO is an AI-powered, open-source video editing software that simplifies tasks like adding text and changing the colour scheme, etc. Its libre nature allows users to freely use, modify, and share the software, promoting collaboration and continuous improvement.

No download needed

AIDO is a free, web-based AI video editor that doesn't require any downloads or installations. Its open-source and libre nature makes it fully customizable, allowing users to edit videos directly in their browser without the need for costly licenses or subscriptions.

The learning curve is less steep

AIDO simplifies video editing with AI and natural language commands (NLP), eliminating the steep learning curve. Users can easily edit videos by typing instructions, letting the AI handle complex tasks for a smooth and intuitive experience.
